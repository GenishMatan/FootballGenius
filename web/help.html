If First time in Football Genius, enter the details in the sign in sector and sign in.
From now, every time you go into Football Genius, Football Genius will automatically recognize you and you will
be connected with your user(this will happen until you clear your browser history, in this case you have the ability to log in(with exist user))

After connecting you will be in the main screen:
just enter a new question with 4 possible answers, mark the right one and click publish question.
Now every Football Genius user will get your question.
to see the questions from other users that waits for you, check the list in the bottom of the screen(will be empty if there is no question waiting).
Select one question from the list and click answer, and you will be directed to the answer page.
In the answer page just mark your guess and click "submit answer", your answer will be sent and you will be back in the main screen. your score will increase if you marked the right answer.

Now just start playing, we got the believe that you got what it takes to be the brightest Football Genius
you can see the top 10 Geniuses by clicking the top ten button
